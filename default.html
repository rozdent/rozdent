COVID-19 Statement

Dear Patients,

COVID-19 has been a very difficult time for all of us and with the shut down of dentistry we understand that many of you have been forced to wait for dental care.

We deeply appreciate your understanding while we were closed to do our part to control the spread of the coronavirus (COVID-19).

We are happy to say that we are planning to reopen our office for patient treatment on June 1, 2020. Our hours of operation will be the same.

Your health and well-being continue to be our highest priority as we carefully prepare and update our practice procedures, as well as train our dental team to do all that we can to minimize the risk of COVID-19 for our patients and team members. Please be assured that we will be taking care of you just as well as we will be taking care of our own families.

We practice family oriented interests in dentistry which include a wide range of services for adults and especially children. Our pleasant and friendly staff is always eager to help accommodate you in any way we can.

Conveniences offered:

  • Flexible hours
  • Day and evening appointments
  • Emergency services
  • New patients always welcome

Financing options:

  • Most dental plans accepted; direct payment from most insurance companies
  • No insurance, no problem: payment plans available
  • Seniors discount
